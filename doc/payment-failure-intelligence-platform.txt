# Payment Failure Intelligence Platform

## Production Ready Agentic AI + RAG Solution

---

# 1. Project Overview

## Project Name

Payment Failure Intelligence Platform

## Domain

ERCOT Generation Interconnection Request (GINR) Operations

## Objective

Build an AI-powered conversational platform that enables operations teams to investigate payment failures, identify root causes, analyze trends, and retrieve historical resolutions using natural language.

---

# 2. Business Problem

ERCOT receives thousands of payment transactions related to:

* Full Interconnection Study (FIS)
* Scoping Study (SS)
* Change Requests
* Analysis Fees
* Interconnection Requests

Operations teams manually review payment reports generated daily.

Problems:

### Manual Investigation

Teams search through hundreds of:

* CSV Reports
* HTML Reports
* Excel Reports
* Email Notifications

to answer simple questions.

Example:

Why did GINR-12345 fail?

### No Historical Intelligence

Finding similar failures requires manual effort.

### Slow Resolution

Support engineers spend hours identifying:

* Root causes
* Previous resolutions
* Payment trends

### Limited Visibility

Management lacks insights into:

* Failure trends
* Most common issues
* Operational bottlenecks

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The platform should:

1. Read all historical payment reports.
2. Store operational knowledge.
3. Answer payment-related questions.
4. Identify root causes.
5. Recommend corrective actions.
6. Generate executive summaries.
7. Analyze payment trends.

---

# 4. Proposed Solution

Build a RAG-based AI platform using:

* LangGraph
* LangChain
* Ollama
* ChromaDB
* FastAPI

The solution transforms historical payment reports into searchable knowledge.

Users can ask:

* Why did GINR-12345 fail?
* Show all duplicate payments.
* What are the top payment failures in the last three months?
* Show payment trends for 2025.
* Which failures are increasing?

Designed and developed an AI-powered Payment Failure Intelligence Platform using LangGraph, LangChain, Ollama, ChromaDB, and FastAPI. Implemented Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines to ingest historical payment reports, perform semantic search, identify root causes, generate recommendations, and provide conversational analytics for ERCOT payment operations. ---

# 20. Interview Explanation (5 Minutes)

The business problem was that ERCOT operations teams manually investigated payment failures using CSV, HTML, and Excel reports. As the volume of reports increased, it became difficult to identify root causes, find similar incidents, and generate management insights.

To solve this, I designed an AI-powered Payment Failure Intelligence Platform using LangGraph, LangChain, Ollama, ChromaDB, and FastAPI. Historical payment reports are ingested through a RAG pipeline, converted into embeddings using the nomic-embed-text model, and stored in ChromaDB. When a user asks a question, a LangGraph workflow orchestrates multiple agents including Search, Root Cause Analysis, Recommendation, and Summary agents. These agents retrieve relevant historical incidents, analyze the issue, recommend actions, and generate human-readable responses.

The platform enables conversational access to operational knowledge, reduces manual investigation effort, improves issue resolution time, and provides management-level insights into payment failure trends and operational risks.
